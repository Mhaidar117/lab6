Our Solar System

Our solar system formed 4.6 billion years ago. There are eight planets in our solar system; Mercury, Venus, Earth, Mars, Jupiter, Saturn, Uranus, and Neptune. Nicolaus Copernicus was first to mathematically prove the heliocentric system of our solar system. The largest astronomical object in our solar system is the Sun, a sphere of plasma which fuses 600 million tons of hydrogen every second.
